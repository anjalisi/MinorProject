Frequently Asked Questions

Login

  1. How do I sign up to the portal?

A: To sign up, click on Login as Student or Recruiter, depending on your user type, and create your account by filling in your details (igdtuw.ac.in domain for Students) and click on Sign Up. Post creating your account, you only need to enter your email and password to login to your dashboard.


  2. How do I reset my password?

A: After navigating to the Login Page, locate the Forgot Password option and enter your email to receive a link to reset your password. Check your email inbox and click on the link received to create a new password.


  3. How do I create my profile?

A: Once you have successfully logged into your Dashboard, click on the Profile tab to view your profile. The Edit button allows you to update the details on your profile. Please make sure ALL the details are correct and up to date before proceeding further.


Students

  4. How do I apply for a company's placement drive?

A: Scrolling on your Student Dashboard leads you to your Notice Board, which contains posts outlining details regarding upcoming recruitment opportunities. Please ensure your profile is up to date before clicking on Register to confirm your application. Kindly register before the mentioned deadline.


  5. What is the eligibility criteria for a particular company?

A: Eligibility criteria may vary with company, constraints may be placed on CGPA, Active and Dead Backs and so on.


  6. Why does my Notice Board indicate that registrations have been closed for the session?

A: Your profile may be blocked in one of two cases: if you have already received an offer or have been debarred from further placements. The Training and Placement Cell reserves the right to block candidates in case of any malpractices or falsification of details. Contact the TnP representative for any further queries.


  7. Can I update my Resume in case of any changes?

A: Simply click on the Edit button on your profile and upload your new resume in pdf format (size should be less than 2 MB) in the Resume section.


  8. How do get my Faculty Letter of Recommendation?

A: Contact the TnP representative and send in your LoR to upload it to your profile and allow companies to view it when you apply for a particular opportunity.


  9. What happens in case a Dream Company visits?

A: In case of a Dream Company, all eligible students are allowed to sit for the placement drive even if they previously have an offer of placement, provided the company has not blocked them for employment.


  10. Can I apply to multiple companies after being shortlisted?

A: Application for further opportunities will be unblocked, if the first offer does not block the candidate, in one of two situations:
 1. A Dream Company visits for recruitment.
 2. The CTC of the second offer is greater than twice the CTC of the first offer.


Recruiters

  11. What is the procedure to Register as a Recruiter?

A: Please follow the following steps to register as a Recruiter for IGDTUW Campus Placement:
 1. Drop an email at tpo@igdtuw.ac.in to contact the TnP Cell.
 2. Fill in the Placement form sent in by the TnP Cell along with necessary documents to receive a formal invitation and your Recruiter ID for the current session.
 3. Create your account on the Recruiter side of the Portal, login to your Profile and complete the details.
 4. Update the status of the recruitment process to the PoC assigned to view Student Applications and conduct recruitment events.


  12. When does placement season start/end?

A: Typically, the Placement season begins with the start of the new Academic Session in IGDTUW which is in the month of August. It continues throughout the year till April which signifies the end of the session.
